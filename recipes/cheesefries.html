Cheese Fries

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces frozen french fries
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup bacon cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup green onions sliced
  • 2 tablespoons parsley chopped
  • 3/4 cup ranch dressing

Instructions

  1. Cook the french fries in the oven on a sheet pan according to package directions.
  2. Remove the pan of fries from the oven. Turn the oven heat to 400 degrees F.
  3. You can finish the fries on a sheet pan, or transfer them to a skillet for a restaurant style presentation.
  4. Sprinkle the cheeses over the fries.
  5. Return the fries to the oven. Bake for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  6. Sprinkle the bacon, green onions and parsley over the fries.
  7. Serve immediately with ranch dressing on the side, or alternatively you can drizzle the ranch over the fries.
